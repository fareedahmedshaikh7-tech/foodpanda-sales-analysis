# foodpanda-sales-analysis

This project analyzes 3 years of food delivery sales data (6,000 orders) to uncover business insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, KPI calculations, and interactive dashboard design).

Analyze revenue trends across months and cities
Identify top-performing food categories
Evaluate payment method preferences
Measure customer churn and cancellation rates
Assess delivery performance impact on ratings

Total Revenue: 14.34M PKR
January recorded highest monthly sales
Multan generated highest revenue
Italian cuisine led in both quantity and revenue
66% of customers preferred digital payments
Cancellation Rate: 32.8%
Churn Rate: 49.7%

Major Finding:
High churn and cancellation rates indicate retention and operational challenges despite stable revenue distribution.
